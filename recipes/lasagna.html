Lasagna alla Bolognese

Lasagna alla Bolognese is a classic Italian baked pasta dish.

Ingredients
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Bolognese ragù
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Grated Parmiggiano Regginao
  • Butter, for greasing the dish and finishing
Procedure
  1. Make the ragù: sauté finely chopped onion, carrot, celery and pancetta in oil/butter; add ground beef and pork, brown, deglaze with wine, add tomato passata and simmer low until thick (2–3 hours).
  2. Make the béchamel: melt butter, stir in flour to form a roux, whisk in warm milk until smooth, season with salt and nutmeg.
  3. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F) and butter a baking dish.
  4. Assemble: layer pasta sheets, ragù, béchamel and grated Parmigiano; repeat for 3–5 layers, finishing with béchamel and cheese.
  5. Bake: 25–40 minutes until golden and bubbling; cover if browning too fast.
  6. Rest 10–15 minutes, slice and serve with extra Parmigiano.
