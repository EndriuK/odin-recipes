Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Roman pasta dish made with eggs, Pecorino Romano, guanciale, and black pepper. It is rich, creamy, and simple.

Ingredients
  • Spaghetti
  • Guanciale
  • Eggs
  • Pecorino Romano
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
Procedure
  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti until al dente.
  2. While the pasta cooks, cut the guanciale into strips and fry it in a pan until crisp.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Pecorino Romano, and black pepper.
  4. Reserve a little pasta water, then drain the spaghetti.
  5. Add the hot pasta to the pan with the guanciale and mix well.
  6. Remove from the heat, then quickly add the egg mixture, stirring constantly to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Add a little pasta water if needed to make it smoother.
  8. Serve immediately with extra Pecorino Romano and black pepper.
